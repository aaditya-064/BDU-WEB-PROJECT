About Us

Buddha Dana Udhyog Pvt. Ltd., a leading name in the feed industry since 2058 B.S. Under the visionary leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Bindu Lal Shrestha, we have evolved from our humble beginnings as Buddha Dana Udhyog to become a trusted provider of premium feed products.

Our extensive product range includes specialized feeds for chickens, fish, cattle, and pigs, catering to the diverse needs of the agricultural sector. We proudly offer our products under three renowned brands: Shraddha, Srijan, and Saurya. For poultry, we provide tailored feeds for broilers, layers, and Giriraj chickens, ensuring optimal nutrition and growth.

Our facilities boast advanced technology, including a state-of-the-art broiler plant that utilizes 100% pure mineral water to generate steam, upholding the highest standards of hygiene and quality in our production process. Additionally, we operate nine well-maintained ponds dedicated to fish hatching, demonstrating our commitment to sustainable aquaculture.

Beyond our core business, we believe in fostering a balanced lifestyle. Our premises include a futsal ground, offering a space for relaxation and entertainment for our employees and the community.

Mr. Bindu Lal Shrestha’s extensive experience, acquired through participation in numerous international events and training programs, has been instrumental in driving our company’s success. His invaluable insights and innovative approach ensure that we produce not just quantitative but qualitative feed products, consistently meeting and exceeding industry standards.

Join us in our journey as we continue to deliver excellence in feed production, driven by a legacy of quality, innovation, and sustainability.
